Safety First

General Lab Safety

  • No running or horseplay
  • Keep workspace clean
  • Ask before using unknown materials
  • Report accidents immediately
  • Know emergency equipment locations

Personal Safety

  • Closed-toe shoes required
  • Safety glasses when working
  • Tie back long hair
  • Remove loose jewelry
  • Appropriate PPE always

Access Requirements

  • Complete appropriate training
  • Open 9:00-5:00 Mon-Fri
  • Never work alone
  • Two trained users minimum
  • Follow MGB R.A.C.E. procedures
